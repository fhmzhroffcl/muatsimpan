# Musim motion deliverables

The storyboard is grounded in `Sources/Musim/Views/`: `DownloadPanel`, `PreDownloadSheet`, `ActivityDock` / `FloatingActivityWindow`, `LibraryView` (Media), and `EditorTabView`. The visual system follows `DesignSystem.swift` (dark OLED background, Senja accent gradient, 16/12/9 radii, system typography).

## Exports

- `renders/musim-launch-landscape.mp4` — 24s, 1920×1080, 60fps
- `renders/musim-launch-vertical.mp4` — 24s, 1080×1920, 60fps
- `renders/features/url-download-flow.mp4` — 10s, landscape, 60fps
- `renders/features/download-progress.mp4` — 10s, landscape, 60fps
- `renders/features/library.mp4` — 10s, landscape, 60fps
- `renders/features/basic-video-editor.mp4` — 10s, landscape, 60fps

`STORYBOARD.md` contains the source-derived scene plan. `TIMELINE.md` contains the audio-marker sheet. `renders/checks/` contains first/middle/final frame checks for both aspect ratios.

The feature cuts are deterministic extracts from the master timeline, with a short final hold where needed to meet the requested 10–15 second feature length.
